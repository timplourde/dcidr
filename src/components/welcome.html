About this app

dcidr helps you make tough decisions involving multiple options and criteria using a simple four-step process.

Give me an example

Decisions like these are difficult because you're pushing the limits of your working memory. This app breaks it down into bite-sized pieces.

Ok, how much?

It's all free as in beer.

How does it work?

You enter your options and criteria then complete a series of sentences. Behind the scenes, dcidr uses a Prioritization Matrix algorithm to rank your options based on your preferences.

Is any of this data being stored?

All data entered only exists in your web browser. No decisions are ever transmitted over the Internet or stored on a server. When you complete a decision, you have the option to save it to your archive (your web browser's localStorage) which you can later purge.

Google Analytics is used to track page views.
